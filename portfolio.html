What is new media art?

What exactly is new media art? Well can definitely say modern tech is part of it? Well by modern technology, generally a huge role for media art, provides for a range of new forms of contemporary art. We tend to equate art as a culture with film, television, and popular music of today. Although these are quite well known, in the last few decades new areas of expression in art have expanded considerably. Many of the New Media Art fields entail kinetic sculpture, which is an art form that involves making robots. Other new media arts into today’s age consist of information art, organic and algorithmic art, digital art, game design, machinima amd many more! Traditionally we tend to believe of art (art in the old days as well) as composed to drawing, watercolor, printing, statue and film photograph. Well into the modern world artist might also include film production production. Computer graphics developments had already ushered digital format based craft into mainstream movie seeking to make to the general public. And this is where I fit into this category. I extremely love capturing the moment whether it’s in a video format or through a photograph. I always think of it a as time travel, since you are capturing a piece of time and looking back it is like. But it’s just called memories in simple terms. I’m not just your simple creator where you think I just take weeding portraits, sports photography, tutoria and prom photos (even though I still do these kinds of gigs for that extra money in my pocket).
